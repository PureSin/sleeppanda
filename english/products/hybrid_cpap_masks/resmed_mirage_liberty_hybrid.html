Mirage Liberty Hybrid Mask



The ResMed Mirage Liberty Oral and Nasal CPAP mask provides the feel and functionality of a full face mask without the size, restraint or leakage found with some traditional full face CPAP masks. To improve patient comfort and compliance, the Liberty CPAP mask includes three sizes of nasal pillows and a small or large oral cushion, allowing the user to select the best fitting sizes at home. The mask is designed for less contact to the upper portion of the face, giving less restriction to your field of vision.


ResMed created this unique design to assist with those patient who have trouble with a traditional full face CPAP mask. This design allows patients to read, watch TV, or wear glasses while having your mask on. ResMed remains a leader in the field of Nasal Interfaces by always allowing the patients to assist in the design of each mask they create.




Features

  • Specially designed headgear provides exceptional comfort while providing optimal stability.
  • Improved vent design disperses air quietly.
  • Mask design provides free field of vision.
