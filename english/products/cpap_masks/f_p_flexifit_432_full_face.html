FlexiFit 432 Full Face





Our second generation Full Face Mask, the FlexiFit 432, takes patient comfort to another level. It incorporates a new Soft Foam Cushion that combines with the contoured Silicone Seal to provide an even better seal and more comfortable fit.





Experience a whole new level of comfort with Fisher & Paykel Healthcare today.





Features:

  • Comes with both Silicone Seal (Small, Medium or Large) and Foam Cushion (Small, Medium or Large) so that you can achieve a more personalized fit.
  • With Foam Cushion FlexiFit Technology automatically adjusts to a wide range of nasal and facial contours with simple one-step ease of fitting and no complicated adjustments.
  • The sliding Glider Strap provides freedom of movement while maintaining a seal. Mask force remains equal when the head rotates from side to side, minimizing the occurrence of leaks.
  • Premium Frosted Seal has a soft, smooth feel to slide onto the face with a frosted texture to optimize the mask seal.
  • A maintenance free advanced air diffuser system prevents air from blowing onto the sleeping partner and decreases noise that could potentially disrupt sleep.
  • An Under-Chin fit provides stability by providing a constant point of reference and maintains the seal even with movement of the jaw during the night.
