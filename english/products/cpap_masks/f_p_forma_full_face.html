Forma Full Face



The Forma Full Face Mask forms a superior seal and provides even more comfort with a greater range of movement through the active contouring of its new FlexiFoam Cushion. The T-Piece adds stability to the stylized Mask Base, and works in harmony with the highly contoured Silicone Seal and Under-Chin Design to enhance sleep performance. With its ease of use and superior conforming seal, the Forma sets a whole new standard.



Transform your patient’s sleep experience today, with the Fisher & Paykel Healthcare Forma Full Face Mask.





Features:

  • FlexiFit Technology
  • Foam Cushion automatically adjusts to a wide range of nasal and facial contours with simple one-step ease of fitting and no complicated adjustments
  • Active Contouring FlexiFoam Cushion
  • provides greater comfort with the unique design of the soft, light and supportive foam. The FlexiFoam features advanced active contouring that conforms naturally to your face
  • Sliding Glider™ Strap with Easy-Release Cord
  • provides freedom of movement and easy pull one step removal. The mask force remains equal when the head rotates from side to side, minimizing leaks
  • Under Chin Design with Crown Strap
  • provides extra stability and maintains the seal, even with movement of the jaw during the night. The Crown Strap works in conjunction with the Under-Chin Design to eliminate the need for a chinstrap.
  • Maintenance-Free Advanced Air Diffuser
  • disperses the airflow away from the sleeper's partner, and minimizes exhaust flow noise
  • T-Piece Forehead Support
  • provides added stability and compliments the Under-Chin Design to allow the mask to auto-adjust for hassle free fitting
  • Frosted Silicone Seal
  • Forma's seal has a soft, smooth feel to slide onto the face and its frosted texture optimizes overall mask seal
  • Four Sizes Available (S, M, L, XL) to fit a wide range of faces comfortably.
  • The Forma uses a single frame size so that any cushion/seal will work on any frame.
