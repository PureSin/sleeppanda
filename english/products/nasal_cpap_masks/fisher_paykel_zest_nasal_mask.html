F&P Zest nasal mask



Compact, quiet and effortless to fit and use, the F&P Zest Nasal Masks offer superb comfort. The innovative design of the Zest Nasal Masks combines the Easy-Clip Silicone Seal with proven technology that makes Fisher & Paykel Healthcare masks perform so well. Zest Q and Lady Zest Q take the best of Zest and add an Advanced Air Diffuser. This refinement ensures minimal noise and efficient exhaust dispersal.


The F&P FlexiFit family (405/406/407) are our original nasal masks, trusted and valued for comfortable FlexiFit technologies and ease of use.





Features:

  • FlexiFit Technology


  • Foam Cushion automatically adjusts to a wide range of nasal and facial contours with simple one-step ease of fitting and no complicated adjustments


  • Sliding Glider™


  • Strap provides freedom of movement while maintaining a secure seal. Mask force remains equal when the head rotates from side to side, minimizing the occurrence of leaks


  • Easy-Clip Silicone Seal


  • Allows one-step connection or removal for quick and effortless cleaning. The new design allows for perfect seal and frame alignment ensuring optimal mask performance with each use


  • Maintenance-Free Advanced Air Diffuser disperses the airflow away from the sleeper's partner, and minimizes exhaust flow noise


  • Stretchgear Headgear made of comfortable and breathable elastic, specially designed to maintain a firm seal


  • Glider Clip & Hidden Strap Release offers simple one-step removal of headgear. No ongoing headgear adjustments required
