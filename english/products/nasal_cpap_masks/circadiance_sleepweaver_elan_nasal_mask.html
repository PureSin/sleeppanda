SleepWeaverElan nasal mask




SleepWeaver Elan combines the proven cloth mask technology of the SleepWeaver Advance Nasal CPAP Mask with a new cushion design that improves seal, increases visibility, and provides sleep position flexibility with a rotating elbow.


The SleepWeaver Elan starter kit includes a regular and a large cushions to ensure the best possible fit. In addition to the mask with two cushions, this bundle also includes the SleepWeaver Feather Weight Tube and Tether Strap extending the option of securing the tube to the headgear. A small sized cushion is available, but sold separately for this starter kit.
