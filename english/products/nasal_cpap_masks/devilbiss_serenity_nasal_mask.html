Serenity Nasal Mask



The serenity nasal mask is lightweight and comfortable to wear. It can be supplied with either gel or silicone cushions and forehead pads, both of which are easily interchangeable. The silicone and gel cushions are available in both standard and shallow sizes.


The headgear is made from soft elasticated fabric and can be easily adjusted to fit securely using the Velcro tabs. Tubing is worn over the head and held in place with a loop attachment on the headgear.


For added comfort and optimal fit, the mask frame can also be moved forwards and backwards to suit the individual user. The Serenity mask also features an adjustable vent to direct air away from the users chest or bed partner.


Features

  • Comfortable headgear is included and is labelled for ease of assembly.
  • Patented adjustable forehead rest takes pressure off the bridge of the nose for maximum patient comfort
  • Snap together components for forehead rest that allow for easy change and no breakage due to complex parts
  • Soft latex free cushion and forehead pads.
  • Adjustable 360° exhaust port allows patient to direct escaping air away from their chest and their bed partner.
  • Solid and reliable thermoplastic frame will hold up to repeated cleanings.
  • Soft, replaceable silicone or gel cushion provides a good seal with low pressure
  • Supplemental oxygen port
