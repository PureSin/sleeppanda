IQ nasal mask



This lightweight nasal mask features a Soft GelTM cushion which fits comfortably against the skin. The IQ mask is available in a single size design as the cushion is mouldable for a more tailored and precise fit.


The IQ mask has been designed to fit comfortably and precisely without the need for additional forehead support and provides an optimum seal.


The headgear is made from a soft, breathable material and can be easily released from the mask during the night.


Features

  • 100% latex free
  • Mouldable soft gel cushion ensures comfortable fit and optimum seal
  • Available as a vented and non-vented version
  • Headgear made from a soft, breathable material
  • Quick release headgear
  • Comfortable and stable fit without additional forehead pad
