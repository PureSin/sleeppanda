FlexSet Nasal Mask



The FlexSet nasal mask features a dual pivoting forehead rest and mask bridge for a more comfortable fit. The forehead pad reduces pressure and eliminates skin indentation and is available as a fabric pad or memory foam option.


The mask is held securely in place with adjustable headgear and can be easily removed during the night using the integrated quick release clip. Cushions are interchangeable, can be supplied as both gel or silicone and available in standard or shallow sizes.


The FlexSet mask also features an adjustable vent which allows the user to direct air away from their chest or bed partner.


Features

  • Mask swivel can rotate 360° or be locked in the over-the-head position for greater flexibility
  • Firm memory foam or ultra soft fabric forehead pad reduces pressure and eliminates skin indentation to ensure comfort
  • Quick-release allows user to detach mask from forehead pad without removing headgear
  • Dual pivoting forehead rest and mask bridge with push-button adjustability - forward and backward
  • Clipless tusk for lower headgear strap allows for easy on/off movement of the mask
  • Self pivoting forehead pad for improved fit and comfort
  • Removable/replaceable cushion.
  • Available in standard and shallow sizes
  • Available with silicon or gel cushions
  • Supplemental oxygen port
