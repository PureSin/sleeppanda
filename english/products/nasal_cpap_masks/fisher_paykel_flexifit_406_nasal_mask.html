F&P FlexiFit 406 Patite nasal mask(Discontinued! Replacement Parts Only)




The Petite design is small, light and easy to use. The Stretchgear™ Headgear provides even greater comfort and the maintenance-free bias diffuser ensures a quieter operation. The FlexiFit 406 Petite Nasal Mask comes as an Out-of-Box Solution fully assembled and ready for use.











Features:

  • FlexiFit's foam cushions are able to conform to a wide range of face shapes


  • Single size frame comes with 2 cushions (SmallMedium and MediumLarge) to fit wide range of faces


  • Cushion design technology providing an increased range of movement at the bridge of the nose • Locking forehead rest helps you 'Fit and Forget' your mask


  • Front swivel moves freely and allows a wide range of tube positioning


  • Glider mechanism creates stability and allows the headgear to move independently of the mask


  • Open eyelets at the forehead rest allow the headgear to be completely removed without having to alter any individual settings – i.e., when washing your headgear


  • Improved diffuser system provides a quiet mask and prevents air blowing on the bed partner


  • The sleek design has been recessed into the elbow of the mask for secure attachment with aesthetic appeal.
