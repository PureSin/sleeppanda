Respironics System One Heated Tubing


Replacement heated tubing for use with the Respironics 560 series of CPAP machines. The System One Heated tubing is answer for patients who are in need of elevated humidification exclusive of condensation.


Cleaning

Daily:
    Tubing should disconnected from your CPAP or BiPAP to be hung in a clean, dry area after each use.

Weekly:

  • Disconnect tubing from CPAP or BiPAP unit as well as the mask.
  • Using a mild detergent with warm water run the mixture through the tubing.
  • After the wash, rinse the tubing thoroughly.
  • Hang the tubing in a clean, dry area to dry. Do not place in direct sunlight.
