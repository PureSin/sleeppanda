ResMed CPAP Batteries


Universal CPAP Battery Pack for philips Respironics
The Battery can be charged in other countries that use 220v by using a 110v-220v converter plug.


The battery can be used for the following length of time based on your machine:

  • DS250HS at 12cm of water pressure for 7-10 Hours with humidifier.
  • DS450HS at 12cm of water pressure for 7-10 Hours with humidifier.
  • DS650HS for 7-10 Hours with Humidifier.
  • DS750HS for 7-10 Hours with Humidifier.
  • REMstar Plus M-Series CPAP Machines at the pressure of 12cm of water pressure for 13 Hours with Humidifier and 20 Hours without Humidifier.
  • REMstar Plus M-Series Bi-Levels for 13 Hours with Humidifier and 20 Hours without Humidifier.
  • REMstar Legacy Machines should get no more than 7-8 Hours of use.


Features of the Respironics Universal Portable Battery Pack:

  • The Universal Portable Battery Pack is the ultimate in power portability and convenience. At 14 pounds, this rechargeable battery is lighter and more compact than traditional gel cell, marine-type batteries that can weigh as much as 50 pounds.
  • The new Universal Portable Battery Pack has a 14.4 amps. per hour rating and it comes with a one year warranty.
  • For use with Respironics M Series Humidifiers (RES-1022257 and RES-1029090).
  • Includes carry case with battery, user guide, and charger with power cord
