H5i Heated Humidifier Chamber: Cleanable


This is a cleanable water chamber used as a long-lasting replacement to the standard H5i Heated Humidifier Water Chamber.


The standard H5i cannot be taken apart and is recommended to be replaced every 6 months. The cleanable chamber can disassemble for cleaning and should last beyond 6 months.


The Chamber will hold 380 ml of water (when properly filled to the fill line). Chamber should be filled with clean water each night. Using distilled water is not required, but it will maximize the life of the chamber and reduce mineral deposits.
