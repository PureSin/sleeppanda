Contour Citrus Scented CPAP Mask Wipe


Contour CPAP Mask Wipes are 100% cotton wipes specially formulated to naturally clean and protect your CPAP equipment without the use of harmful chemicals. Contour Wipes effectively remove dirt, grease, oils and other organic residue from your masks and equipment, keeping them clean and fresh. The special 100% natural formula is enhanced with a gentle citrus scent for a fresh, clean feeling.


Keep your mask clean the safe and easy way! Contour Wipes are alcohol free, latex free, and biodegradable. They are designed to effectively clean ALL types of CPAP, BIPAP/Bilevel and Oxygen equipment (including masks, tubing, and accessories) without cracking or hardening.
