REMATEE Bumper Belt



REMATEE Bumper Belt is a positional sleep aid that is designed to help patients remain in a side-sleeping position throughout the night. Made with REMATEE's patented bumper system, they are easy to wear over your favorite sleepwear. Available in four sizes to accomodate a large range of users, including children. It is constructed of neoprene for a comfortable feel, and the belt has a super soft adjustable velcro strap for a perfect fit. The inflatable bumpers are easy to use, compact well for travelling, and are also super strong.
