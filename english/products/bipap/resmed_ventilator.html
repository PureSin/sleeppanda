Stellar™ 100 Ventilator



  • Protect every breath

    The Stellar™ 100 ventilator is designed for use across different hospital settings where non-dependent patients are on the move. Lightweight, small, quiet and versatile, the Stellar 100 ventilator provides high-quality performance for a wide range of adult and pediatric (> 13 kg) patients using invasive and noninvasive applications.

    With easy access and quality performance features, the Stellar 100 ventilator offers advanded settings that are optimized, allowing medical staff to focus on and fine-tune the pressure settings required for each individual patient.


  • Features

    User interface is simple with intuitive menus, easy navigation, user-friendly push dial, integrated humidification, internal battery and USB convenience for data capturing.

    The Stellar 100 ventilator protects every breath through advanced leak compensation with Vsync and enhanced patient-ventilator synchrony with TiControl™ technology.
