IntelliPAP® Bilevel S System



With a minimalistic design, small footprint, quiet operation, and SmartCode technology, the DeVilbiss IntelliPAP Bilevel S will provide you with a machine that is sure to meet and exceed your needs.

The IntelliPAP Bilevel S is the perfect choice for patients who require higher pressures. With a pressure range of 3 to 25 cm H2O and the ability to adjust the IPAP and EPAP delta up to 22 cm H2O, the IntelliPAP Bilevel S offers maximum adjustability and can be operated in CPAP and Bilevel mode.

The IntelliPAP Bilevel also has an exclusive Flow-Rounding technology that softens transitions from IPAP to EPAP pressures. By selecting a setting 1 to 5 you are able to select the sensitivity of your Flow-Rounding.

Your IntelliPAP Bilevel S will score and record your breathing patterns detecting apneas, hypopneas and non-responding events. You can retrieve an alpha-numeric code from your machine and use that code on the DeVilbiss Healthcare website to "decode" it and generate your free therapy report. You can find out more information about SmartCode here. You can also view your actual pressure, leak rate, and breathing parameters such as breath rate, I/E ratio, respiratory events, and minute volume on the large back lit LDC display on your machine.

At 26 dBA this bilevel is among the quietest available on the market today. With additional features like delay, heated humidifier, automatic altitude adjustment, auto on/off, no power brick, and reminder alerts for part replacement, the IntelliPAP Bilevel S is a great value.


Features

  • Exclusive Flow-Rounding technology softens transitions from IPAP to EPAP pressure
  • Respiratory Event Detection algorithm scores patient breathing patterns detecting apneas, hypopneas and non-responding events. Reports generated by SmartLink® or SmartCode®
  • LCD display shows actual pressure, leak rate and patient breathing parameters such as breath rate, I/E ratio, respiratory events, minute volume and tidal volume
  • Ramp delay settings of 0 to 45 minutes in 5-minute increments
  • Among the quietest bilevel units on the market
  • Integrated heated humidification
  • Exclusive humidifier chamber design maximizes humidification
  • Automatic Altitude Adjustment up to 8,500 feet
  • Includes exclusive SmartCode technology
