F&P Opus 360 Nasal Pillow Mask



The slim-line design allows for almost any sleeping position without mask displacement. Its innovative 360-degree pivoting ball-and-socket elbow provides greater versatility. With its new Silicone Pillows and facially Contoured Mask Frame for extra stability, patient comfort is paramount.


Elegant in design and simplicity, let the Opus 360 Nasal Pillows Mask from Fisher & Paykel Healthcare work for you today.




Features:

  • The anatomically shaped pillows provide easy fitting with an excellent seal. The contours of the nasal pillows ensure greater comfort with an "off the lip" seal. The range of sizes provided (S,M,L) ensures comfort and seal are not compromised.


  • The facially contoured mask frame features smooth, soft pads with three-dimensional support structures. It conforms naturally to the shape of the face to provide a comfortable fit and reliable support. Its slim-line design allows side-sleeping without mask displacement.


  • Wide Range of Pillow Sizes is Provided - The contours of the nasal pillows ensure greater comfort and the range of sizes (Small, Medium & Large) provided ensures comfort and seal are not compromised.


  • Tube Anchoring System - The innovative pivot system allows the tube to be anchored at the top or side, or left free to track. Elegant in design and simplicity, it firmly holds the tube in the desired position. Alternatively, the tube can be left unanchored so that the balland-socket elbow allows true 360-degree freedom.


  • Efficient Exhaust - The advanced air diffuser system is quiet, minimizing the noise and draft of the exhaust flow to prevent both sleeper and partner disturbance.


  • Soft-Lip Cushion - The Silicone Seal is soft on the nose and supple on the upper lip. The built-in Soft-Lip™ Cushion conforms naturally to the shape of the upper lip, for maximum comfort.


  • Premium Frosted Silicone Seal - The enhanced seal technology has a soft, smooth feel and slides easily into position. Its frosted texture optimizes mask seal performance.
