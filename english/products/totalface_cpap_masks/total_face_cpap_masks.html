   Respironics FitLife Total Face Mask
The Philips Respironics FitLife "Total" face CPAP mask provides a solution for those who have trouble wearing conventional full face CPAP masks. Facial hair, dentures, nasal bridge breakdown, facial irregularities, and claustrophobia are issues that can greatly impact the ability to establish effective therapy and comfort.
Respironics Total Face CPAP Mask
The Respironics Total Face CPAP Mask is designed for patients who have difficulty tolerating other full face and nasal CPAP masks. Some benefits of the Total Face CPAP Mask are a one size fits most and a fast and effective seal with no fitting required.
