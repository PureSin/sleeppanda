About US

Sleep Panda CPAP Corpis a Canadian company specializing in the provision of CPAP therapy products and services to clients with Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA). We are fully licensed by Assistive Devices Program in Ontario (ADP) which is a program partially covers the expense of CPAP products. Insurance can be used to cover the remaining portion of the cost of the equipment.

We carry all the major manufacturers products including ResMed, Philips Respironics , Fisher&Paykel, Kego and other world leading brands that are approved by ADP.
