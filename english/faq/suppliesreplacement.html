CPAP Supplies Replacement Schedule

CPAP supplies such as masks, tubing, filters and headgear are not designed to last extreme amounts of time, and using them past their recommended life span can actually jeopardize your CPAP therapy. Delicate silicone mask cushions can develop leaks, clogged filters can allow dust and pollen to be delivered into your airway, and over-stretched headgear can require extreme tightening, causing pressure point pain.

Below you will find Recommended Replacement Schedule for CPAP or BiPAP supplies, based on information from insurance companies. Call us today to replace your CPAP supplies!

Item Replacement Frequency
CPAP MaskEvery 6 months
CPAP TubingEvery 12 months
CPAP FilterEvery 3 months
Humidifier ChamberEvery 12 months
CPAP Chin StrapEvery 12 months
CPAP Machine Every 5 years or as necessary
